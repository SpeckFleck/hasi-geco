Simulating Hard Spheres in Geometric Confinement

hasi-geco provides a configuration space for mocasinns. It helps you to simulate Hard Sphere liquids or serves as a good starting point for developing your own Hard Sphere simulations with mocasinns.

What works?

  • 3D Hard Spheres in Confinement
  • Available Confinements: spheres, cylinders, plates, nodal approximations of periodic p, d, g and iwp-surfaces - all from inside and outside

What's to come?

  • Rejection free simulation methods
  • Alternative step proposal schemes
